# Assumptions

This proposal is directional. The following assumptions must be reviewed and validated before any execution work begins.

## Data access
- **No internal data access.** All figures, benchmarks, and estimates are external or inferred.
- No CRM, analytics, or attribution systems were queried during authorship.
- All customer, revenue, and performance figures referenced are placeholders pending validation.

## Strategic direction
- The recommendations here are **directional strategy only**.
- They represent a defensible starting point, not a committed plan.
- Specific channels, budgets, and tactics are illustrative.

## Validation required post-approval
Before launch, the following require client or stakeholder confirmation:
- Target segments and ICP
- Budget envelope and channel mix
- KPI definitions and success thresholds
- Brand/voice guardrails
- Legal, privacy, and compliance constraints
- Internal team capacity and delivery ownership
- Martech stack and tracking capabilities

## Scope caveats
- Creative execution, legal review, and localization are out of scope for this proposal.
- Anything not explicitly listed in `SCOPE.md` should be treated as excluded.
